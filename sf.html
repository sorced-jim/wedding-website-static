There is lots to do in and around San Francisco. Here are some of the things we've enjoyed that we think you will too.

  • Napa wine tour–Take the ferry to Vallejo from San Francisco. Then take a limousine around Napa Valley for a 6 hour tour.
  • Sonoma Wine tour–Limousine from San Francisco for an 8 hour tour.
  • Exporatorium–Sonia and Jim met here!
  • Pier 39 Aquarium
  • Go car tour
  • Ghirardelli square
  • Fishermen's Wharf
  • Golden Gate Park
  • Legion of Honor
  • California Academy of Science
  • Cliff House / Beach Chalet–Near San Francisco's Ocean Beach & the old windmills.
  • Fortune Cookie Factory
  • Big Basin State Park–South of San Francisco with lots of Redwoods.
  • Winchester Mystery House–Down in San Jose, Mimi loved this place.
  • Dr. Seuss Art Museum
  • SF Railway Museum
