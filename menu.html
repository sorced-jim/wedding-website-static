Our wedding is being catered by Chef Stephanie. We have picked out the following menu:

  • Lamb and feta kebabs with roasted red pepper sauce
  • Mini seafood tostadas, salsa fresca, avocado cream, (GF)
  • Vegan skewers: peppers, eggplant, onion, zucchini, mushrooms, (V)
  • Chicken dumplings: ground chicken, spinach, carrots, onion, ginger, celery with mint chili sauce
  • Coriander crusted scallops chive potato hash & corn relish (GF)
  • Oriechiete with arugula pesto, cherry tomatoes, asiago, pecans (V)
  • Grass fed beef sliders, carmelized onion, chipotle aioli
  • Heirloom tomatoes with fresh mozzarella, lentils, white balsamic, olive oil, salt/pepper (GF, V)
  • Beer soaked bratwurst skewers, apples, onions, horseradish mustard
  • Chile spiced sweet potatoes sunflower seed ranch dressing (GF, V)

GF = Gluten Free, V = Vegan (may have cheese on the side).

Cocktail service will include various beer, wine, champagne, non-alcoholic beverages, and our signature cocktail, the cosmopolitan.

A coffee and tea service will be provided as well.

We will be serving San Francisco's famous Dynamo donuts for dessert.

  • Spiced chocolate: chocolate donut coated in sugar, cinnamon, chile, and chipotle
  • Passion Fruit Milk Chocolate: vanilla donut glazed with passionfruit and a touch of milk chocolate shavings
